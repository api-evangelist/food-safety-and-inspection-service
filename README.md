# Food Safety and Inspection Service (food-safety-and-inspection-service)
The Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) is a branch of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) responsible for ensuring the safety of the nation's commercial supply of meat, poultry, and egg products.
